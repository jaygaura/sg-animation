Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Srila Bhaktivedanta Narayana Goswami Maharaja  •  100th Anniversary

It just so happened that due to some circumstances for many of us it might not be possible to gather and celebrate this wonderful day in great numbers, as otherwise we would all definitely love.

With this in mind we created a little challenge for you, and a way for all of us to still have a great celebration… TOGETHER!

You likely already know that in our Gaudiya Tradition there is a proper sadhana, a proper way of doing pretty much anything and everything. It's the one that will grant us the most benefit. So, of course, there is also a way to properly honor our Guru-varga and, especially, our dearmost Srila Gurudeva on this special day.

Instead of just "stuffing" the website with a whole bunch of content and leaving you "along" with that to try to go through and digest all the information, we've created an ecstatic guided tour for you. It's going to take as little as 10 minutes or as much time as you will want to take, exploring all the nectar, and consciously pleasing your Srila Gurudeva through your heart's expression– Hopefully we will all do this, and be united– and will HOPEFULLY give much pleasure to our Srila Gurudeva in this way.

So, are you ready?
